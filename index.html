What is Ludeo?

Ludeo is an innovative platform that transforms captured gaming moments into standalone gameplay units that can be shared on social media platforms or websites for others in the gaming community to relive the highlighted moment. Additionally, Ludeo can create a challenge for your community, which can boost your game's engagement, retention and KPIs.

Ludeo Architecture

Ludeo is comprised of three essential components that form its structure:

Ludeo Tracker

Ludeo Tracker is responsible for recording and saving the game data from your gaming moments. This data, including videos and images, creates the Ludeo, a standalone gameplay unit. Ludeo Tracker also assists the Creator Lab in defining the crucial elements of your Ludeo.

Creator Lab

The Creator Lab is a vital platform within Ludeo that enables users to configure their gaming moments or set conditions for their Ludeo gameplay units. This includes defining the objective or goal of the Ludeo, setting the moment's duration, establishing scoring parameters, and defining the name and description of the Ludeo. Once the Ludeo is created, it can be published on social media platforms to be shared with others in the gaming community.

Ludeo Replayer

The Ludeo Replayer is responsible for creating the Ludeo file and opening it within the selected game. It is also responsible for the Ludeo GUI, facilitating communication between the Ludeo architecture, calculating the Ludeo score, and generating leaderboards.

Play Ludeo

To take advantage of Ludeo, users need to integrate Ludeo SDK and enable Ludeo Generation on their chosen game.

Capture your Moment

To utilize Ludeo, the first step is to capture your gaming moment using the Ludeo Tracker on your selected game.

Shared your Moment

Secondly, you can share your captured gaming moment on social media platforms to challenge the gaming community to re-experience your moment with Ludeo's link. You can customize the configuration for your moment on the Ludeo Creator Lab.

Play the Moment

Lastly, other gamers can play the exact gaming moment you shared by clicking the Ludeo links. This will open up the respective game on the Steam game launcher, utilizing the Ludeo Replayer.
