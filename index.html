Introduction

The Ludeo SDK for Unity is a powerful tool for game studios that enables players to generate a new form of gaming content by capturing and sharing their highlight moments within a game, which other players can discover and replay. The SDK is simple to install and configure, offering many features for enhancing user engagement and community development.

Only whitelisted Ludeo Partner can use and access the Ludeo SDK. For further information, please contact us.

What is Ludeo?

Ludeo is an innovative platform that converts captured gaming moments into standalone gameplay units. The following are the basic features of Ludeo:

  1. Capture Your Moment: Use the Ludeo Tracker to capture your gaming moment in your selected game.
  2. Share Your Moment: Share your captured gaming moment on social media platforms as a challenge for the gaming community to re-experience your moment with the Ludeo link. You can configure your moment on the Ludeo Creator Lab.
  3. Play the Moment: Fellow gamers can play the exact gaming moment that you have shared by clicking the Ludeo link. This will open up the respective game on the Steam game launcher through the use of Ludeo Replayer.

Ludeo High-Level Parts

Ludeo has three high-level parts:

Ludeo Tracker

Ludeo Tracker is responsible for recording and saving the game data from your gaming moments. This data, including videos and images, creates the Ludeo, a standalone gameplay unit. Ludeo Tracker also assists the Creator Lab in defining the crucial parameters such as the Goals, End conditions, and Scoring parameters of your Ludeo.

Creator Lab

The Creator Lab is a vital platform within Ludeo that enables users to configure their gaming moments or set conditions for their Ludeo gameplay units. This includes defining the objective or goal of the Ludeo, the end conditions of the gameplay, setting the gameplay's duration, establishing scoring parameters, and defining the name and description of the Ludeo. Once the Ludeo is created, it can be published on social media platforms to be shared with others in the gaming community.

Ludeo Replayer

The Ludeo Replayer is responsible for creating and opening the Ludeo file within the selected game. It is also responsible for the Ludeo's GUI, facilitating communication between the Ludeo architecture, calculating the Ludeo score, and generating leaderboards.

How It Works?

Ludeo creates LudeoDK, which stands for the Ludeo Development Kit. This facilitates the integration between the Ludeo system and the chosen game, allowing Ludeo to recreate the highlighted moment within the game. LudeoDK was created through the use of a Software Development Kit.

Why Use SDK?

SDK enables Ludeo to construct the LudeoDK application, which functions as a toolkit and enables developers to integrate with the chosen game. LudeoDK is exclusive to Ludeo and enables users to recollect, recreate, and experience the exact highlight moment of other players.

Benefits of SDK

Ludeo utilizes SDK based on the following factors:

  • Easy Integration: By utilizing the LudeoDK, the integration process is sped up because it simplifies integrating with the Ludeo technology infrastructure and requires minimal maintenance.
  • Performance: LudeoDK enhances the experience without affecting your game's FPS, allowing you to relive the gaming moment without FPS drops or latency.
  • Privacy: Using GDPR Compliance and COPPA Compliance, LudeoDK also helps you maintain your privacy.
  • Security: Using MD5 Encryption, RSA, PEM Keys, HTTPS, and SSL/TLS to maintain security, LudeoDK is extremely secure.

How Ludeo Works

In general, Ludeo works as portrayed in the image above. However, these actions are performed by three stakeholders: Game Developer, Game Creator, and Player.

Step 1: Integrate your Game

As the Game Developer, you must integrate your game into Ludeo and configure the necessary parameters and data so that Ludeo can generate autonomous game units that allow other players to replay a creator's gameplay on your game.

Step2: The Creator Makes a Ludeo

The creator plays the game while the Ludeo tracker is activated to record the gameplay and collect all necessary data. The creator will then configure Ludeo's goals and score parameters on the Creator Lab platform. The creator shares the Ludeo to Social Media as links and on the Play Ludeo platform.

Step 3: The Player Plays the Ludeo

The Player clicks the links or the play button, is redirected to the integrated game, and plays the game in the same environment as the creator's gameplay with additional gamification layers that display the end conditions, goals, and score parameters that are used to calculate the player's score.

The Creator captures the gameplay, and the Game Tracker will send it to the Game Manager for scoring criteria when modifying the gameplay on Creator Lab to create the Ludeo for the Ludeo Manager. Ludeo Manager creates Ludeo based on the creator's configuration in the Creator Lab, turns it into a link or uploads it to Ludeo Discovery. Players click links or Ludeo Play buttons to start the Ludeo Player on the integrated game to recreate the creator's Ludeo. The Game tracker will process and store player info in Ludeo Game Manager.

Game Tracker

The creator can activate the Game tracker upon integration with your game to process data states in order to track the player's objectives and score parameters. Game tracker also gathers the Player's data when the player replays the creator's Ludeo. The data is then transmitted to the Game Manager in the Ludeo Backend Services for storage.

Media Uploader

Upon integration, once the gameplay has been recorded, the Media Uploader will send all media to the Ludeo Backend Services, where it will be stored in the Game Manager.

Game Manager

Game Manager serves as a repository for a Ludeo session's essential information and recorded media. The data will then be transmitted to the Creator Lab.

Creator Lab

This is the platform where the creator configures their session's Goals and Scoring parameters.

Ludeo Manager

The configured Ludeo is then stored on the Ludeo Manager and converted into hyperlinks that can be published to the Ludeo Discovery Platform or shared on social media platforms. The data is also sent to the Ludeo Player so that the creator's gameplay can be replayed.

Ludeo Player

The configured Ludeo is transformed into a standalone game device that operates within the game that has been incorporated with the Ludeo system. Ludeo Player will generate an environment that enables the player to reenact the exact same scene, including any objectives and score parameters that the creator had configured.
