What is Ludeo?

Ludeo is an innovative platform that transforms captured gaming moments into standalone gameplay units that can be shared on social media platforms or websites for others in the gaming community to relive the highlighted moment. Additionally, Ludeo can create a challenge for your community, which can boost your game's engagement, retention and KPIs.

Ludeo High-Level Parts

Ludeo is comprised of three high-level parts:

Ludeo Tracker

Ludeo Tracker is responsible for recording and saving the game data from your gaming moments. This data, including videos and images, creates the Ludeo, a standalone gameplay unit. Ludeo Tracker also assists the Creator Lab in defining the crucial parameters such as Goals, End conditions, and Scoring parameters of your Ludeo.

Creator Lab

The Creator Lab is a vital platform within Ludeo that enables users to configure their gaming moments or set conditions for their Ludeo gameplay units. This includes defining the objective or goal of the Ludeo, the end conditions of the gameplay, setting the gameplay's duration, establishing scoring parameters, and defining the name and description of the Ludeo. Once the Ludeo is created, it can be published on social media platforms to be shared with others in the gaming community.

Ludeo Replayer

The Ludeo Replayer is responsible for creating and opening the Ludeo file within the selected game. It is also responsible for the Ludeo GUI, facilitating communication between the Ludeo architecture, calculating the Ludeo score, and generating leaderboards.

Ludeo Parameters

Ludeo is not merely a recreation of gameplay; additional gamification components, such as end conditions, objectives, and scoring parameters, can be added to make it engaging and entertaining.

End Conditions

End condition parameters are predefined parameters that determine the boundaries of a Ludeo within your game. For instance, The Ludeo ends if the player dies, if the time limit is reached, if all enemies are eliminated, or if the conditions are met.

Attention
End Conditions is a predefined parameter that cannot be changed by the Ludeo Creator.

Goals

Goals is an object the creator can specify by selecting from a list of predefined goals provided by Ludeo based on the gameplay. The objectives may also be a constant condition specified by the creator. By configuring the Goals, the gameplay of Ludeo can be adjusted, encouraging players to play.

Score Parameters

Score Parameters enhance the competitiveness and re-playability of the gameplay. Creator may assign a Ludeo up to four active score parameters. The game developer can define up to 20 unique score parameters.

Play Ludeo

Play Ludeo is a website that contains the all of Ludeo's content. Play Ludeo functions as a social media platform that is exclusive to Ludeo content. You can discover many game highlights for a variety of game categories and challenges in order to relive the highlight moment quickly and easily.

Attention
All developers are strongly encouraged to engage with the Ludeo experience in Play Ludeo.

Stake Holders

Stakeholders are interested parties who will interact mainly with Ludeo; there are three categories of Stakeholders in Ludeo:

Game Developer

Game Developer is responsible for integrating the game with Ludeo and defining all necessary data and parameters. To incorporate your game with Ludeo, kindly refer here.

Creator

Any participant who participates in the game can create Ludeo. Creators are the individuals who create Ludeo by capturing their gameplay, defining the end conditions, objectives, and score parameters, and then publishing it on social media platforms and the Play Ludeo website.

User/Player

Users or players are the individuals who play Ludeo. They click the link or the Play button to re-experience the creator's gameplay.
