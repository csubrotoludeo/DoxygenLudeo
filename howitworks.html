How It Works?

Ludeo creates LudeoDK, which stands for the Ludeo Development Kit. This facilitates the integration between the Ludeo system and the chosen game, allowing Ludeo to recreate the highlighted moment within the game. LudeoDK was created through the use of a Software Development Kit.

Warning
Only whitelisted Ludeo partners and those who accept the Integration Terms & Conditions are permitted to integrate with Ludeo and enable the Ludeo Generation on their Game. For further information, please contact us.

Why use SDK?

SDK enables Ludeo to construct the LudeoDK application, which functions as a toolkit and enables developers to integrate with the chosen game. LudeoDK is exclusive to Ludeo and enables users to recollect, recreate, and experience the exact highlight moment of other players.

Benefits of SDK

Ludeo utilizes SDK based on the following factors:

  • Easy Integration: By utilizing the LudeoDK, the integration process is sped up because it simplifies integrating with the Ludeo technology infrastructure and requires minimal maintenance.
  • Performance: LudeoDK enhances the experience without affecting your game's FPS, allowing you to relive the gaming moment without FPS drops or latency.
  • Privacy: Using GDPA Compliance and COPPA Compliance, LudeoDK also helps you maintain your privacy.
  • Security: Using MD5 Encryption, RSA, PEM Keys, HTTPS, and SSL/TLS to maintain security, LudeoDK is extremely secure.

How Ludeo Works

In general, Ludeo works as portrayed in the image above. However, these actions are performed by three stake holders: Game Developer, Game Creator, and Player.

Step 1: Integrate your Game

As the Game Developer, you must integrate your game into Ludeo and configure the necessary parameters and data so that Ludeo can generate autonomous game units that allow other players to replay a creator's gameplay on your game.

Step2: The Creator Makes a Ludeo

The creator plays the game while the Ludeo tracker is activated to record the gameplay and collect all necessary data. The creator will then configure Ludeo's goals and score parameters on the Creator Lab platform. The creator shares the Ludeo to Social Media as links and on the Play Ludeo platform.

Step 3: The Player Plays the Ludeo

The Player clicks the links or the play button, is redirected to the integrated game, and plays the game in the same environment as the creator's gameplay with additional gamification layers that display the end conditions, goals, and score parameters that are used to calculate the player's score.

Ludeo Architecture

Tracking Your Game

The creator can activate the Game tracker upon integration with your game to process data states in to track the player's objectives and score parameters. The data is then transmitted to the Ludeo Backend Services Game Manager for storage.

Uploading Gameplay Media

Upon integration, once the gameplay has been recorded, the Media Uploader will send all media to the Ludeo Backend Services, where it will be stored in the Game Manager.

Game Manger

Game Manager serves as a repository for a Ludeo session's essential information and recorded media. The data will then be transmitted to the Creator Lab.

Creator Lab

This is the platform where the creator configures their session's Goals and Scoring Parameters.

Ludeo Manger

The configured Ludeo is then stored on Ludeo Manager and converted into Hyperlinks that can be published to the Ludeo Discovery Platform or shared on social media platforms. The data is also sent to the Ludeo Player so that the creator's gameplay can be replayed.

Ludeo Player

The configured Ludeo is transformed into a standalone game device that operates within the game that has been incorporated with the Ludeo system. Ludeo Player will generate an environment that enables the player to reenact the exact same scene, including any objectives and score parameters that the creator had configured.
